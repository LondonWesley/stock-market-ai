About Us

Vision: The vision of the project is to create a stock market predictor to help our customer make smarter investments decisions. More and more people are getting interested in investing but have difficulty trusting stock market brokers. To help facilitate our customers make investments on their own.

Mission: Futurevest will be an artificial intelligence algorithm that will follow the basic trend of the stock market to help facilitate our customers decisions in what they invest. Futurevest will use elastic net regression,supported by linear regression, lasso regression and ridge regression. The algorithm results will be accessible for view through our Futurevest website, where they are able to see graphs displaying the trend of a select group of stocks and the prediction made by the AI algorithm.

Intended Audience: This product is for new and experienced investors to get a basic understanding of what will happen to the stock market before it happens.
